Gus was a small three year old boy who stayed with his grandma while his parents were away. His adventure started one sunny day when he went out to visit the museum. He had heard a lot of stories about the museum. Stories of wonderful objects and strange histories.
Gus arrived at the museum with excitement, only to find that it was closed. He was so disappointed and felt bitter about having to wait for it to open. As he sat outside, he noticed an old man limping nearby.
When Gus asked why the old man was limping, he said it was from a fight he had many years ago. This sparked a conversation between the two of them, and the old man soon revealed how he had been coming to the museum every day, since the fight had taken place.
When Gus asked why, the old man sadly said that he was looking for peace. He was hoping his story would be written down in the museum so that he could find forgiveness. 
Gus offered to help the old man, and when the museum opened, they both went inside. One of the museum curators was so impressed with the old man's story that he agreed to write it down in the museum's history.
The old man smiled with relief and thanked Gus. It was a long journey, but the arrival of the old man's story brought peace to him and a happy ending for Gus.
