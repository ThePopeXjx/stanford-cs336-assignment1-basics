Once upon a time, there was a silly dog named Max. Max liked to mix things up. He would mix his toys, his food, and even his clothes. Max was very silly, but everyone loved him.
One day, Max was playing in the park. He saw a big box with lots of colors. Max thought it would be fun to mix all the colors together. He put his paw in the box and started to mix. The colors mixed and made a new color. Max was very happy.
Max showed his new color to his friends. They all laughed and thought it was a very silly color. They played in the park with the new color and had lots of fun. Max was happy that he could mix things up and make everyone smile.
