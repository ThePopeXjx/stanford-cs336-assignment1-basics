Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Tim. He loved to play with his toys all day. His favorite toy was a small ball with a spike. Tim was never fearful when he played with his spiky ball. He would throw it, catch it, and roll it around the room.
One day, Tim's mom said, "Be careful, Tim! The spike on your ball can be sharp. Don't play too rough with it." Tim listened to his mom, but he still liked to play with his ball.
The next day, Tim was playing with his ball, and he accidentally threw it too hard. The ball hit the wall, and the spike made a hole. Tim felt fearful as he looked at the hole. He remembered what his mom said and knew he should have been more careful.
Tim went to his mom and said, "Mom, I made a hole in the wall with my ball. I'm sorry." His mom hugged him and said, "It's okay, Tim. We can fix the hole. Just remember to be more careful when you play with your spiky ball."
From that day on, Tim was more careful when he played with his spiky ball. He learned that being careful and listening to his mom was important. And they all lived happily ever after.
