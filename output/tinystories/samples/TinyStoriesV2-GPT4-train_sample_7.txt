One day, a little otter was playing by the river. He loved to swim and keep his fur clean. He was a very happy otter. But one day, he saw a big fish in the water. The big fish wanted to play too, but the little otter was scared. He did not want to play with the big fish.
The little otter started to cry. He did not know what to do. His mom heard him cry and came to see what was wrong. She saw the big fish and told the little otter not to be scared. She said, "Big fish can be nice too. We can all play together and have fun."
So, the little otter, his mom, and the big fish all played together in the river. They had a lot of fun and the little otter learned that he should not be scared of new friends. The moral of the story is to be brave and give new friends a chance.
