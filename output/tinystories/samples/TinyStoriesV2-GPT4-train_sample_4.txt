Once upon a time, in a big forest, there was a wise old owl. He had many friends, but they did not play together. One day, the wise owl had an idea. He wanted to unite all his friends to have fun.
The wise owl talked to his friend, the little rabbit. "Let's unite all our friends and play a big game," he said. The little rabbit liked the idea and hopped away to tell the others.
In the forest, there was a big tree. The wise owl knew that something fun would happen there soon. All the friends met at the big tree, just like the wise owl said. They played and laughed together, happy to be united. The wise owl watched them with a big smile, knowing he had made his friends happy.
