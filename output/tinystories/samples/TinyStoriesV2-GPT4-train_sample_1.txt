Once upon a time, there was a warm little house. In the house lived a kind man and a sweet dog. The man loved the dog and the dog loved the man.
One day, they went for a walk. The dog saw a cat and ran after it. The man called the dog to come back, but the dog did not listen. He ran and ran until he was far away. The man was sad and looked for the dog.
The dog found himself in a place with a big fence. It was a jail. The dog did not like the jail. He wanted to go back to the warm house. The man found the dog and said, "Do not run away again." The dog knew he should listen. They went back to the warm house, and the dog never ran away again. He would always repeat to himself, "Stay with the man."
