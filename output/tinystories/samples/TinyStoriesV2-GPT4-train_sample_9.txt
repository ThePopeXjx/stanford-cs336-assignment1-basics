Once upon a time, in a big forest, there was a strong lion named Leo. Leo had a favorite spot under a big tree where he liked to rest. One day, he saw a little mouse named Max running by. Max was trying to manage to get some food for his family.
"Hi there, Max!" said Leo. "What are you doing?"
Max looked up at Leo and said, "Hi, Leo! I am trying to manage to get food for my family, but it's hard."
Leo wanted to help Max. He used his strong paws to help Max get the food. Together, they managed to get a lot of food for Max's family. Max was very happy and thanked Leo.
From that day on, Leo and Max became best friends. They always helped each other and enjoyed playing at their favorite spot under the big tree.
