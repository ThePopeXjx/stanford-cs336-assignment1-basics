Once there was a young girl, named Anna. She was three years old and lived with her parents by the river. 
One day, Anna's parents said they were going on vacation and would have to leave her at home. Anna felt sad and lonely. 
At the same time, Anna noticed a group of ducks by the river. The ducks were all running and racing across the river, having so much fun! 
Anna called out to the ducks, “Would you like to race with me?”
The ducks stopped and replied, “Oh yes! We’d love to.”
So, Anna and the ducks raced each other across the river. As they raced, Anna forgot all about being sad and lonely. She had so much fun playing with the ducks! 
When Anna’s parents came back from vacation, they were surprised to see Anna playing with the ducks.
Moral: It is never too soon for Anna to make new friends, no matter how lonely she may feel.
